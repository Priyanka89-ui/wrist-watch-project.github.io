Questions on COD

What is cash on delivery payment option?

Cash on Delivery is a mode of payment provided by Titan where you can pay in cash upon the receipt of your product. We do not take any advance payment for COD orders

International Shipping

US | UK | UAE | Australia | Singapore

For all international orders, your cart value should be minimum of $200 and maximum of $10000, which is inclusive of all shipping and handling charges

Encircle

What is Encircle Program?

Encircle is Kairo’s Unified Loyalty program which allows members to earn & redeem points across all Titan brand stores & at www.kairowatches.in. Every customer who shops at any of the following Titan showrooms & www.kairowatches.in becomes an encircle member - Tanishq, Goldplus, MIA, Zoya, Helios,Kairo Eyeplus..
